### Add Surface Tag

Add the below script tag inside your `<head>` tag.

````HTML
<!-- Start of Surface Form snippet -->

<script
    src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/trysurface/scripts@latest/surface_tag.js"
    type="application/javascript"
> </script>

<!-- End of Surface Form snippet -->
````
