The Lead Table provides a comprehensive view of all your leads, including form submissions and website visitors. It automatically enriches and scores leads based on your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).

</Frame>

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Unified Lead View" icon="table">
    See all leads in one place:
    <ol>
        <li>1. Form submissions</li>
        <li>2. Website visitors</li>
        <li>3. De-anonymized leads</li>
        <li>4. Enriched profiles</li>
    </ol>
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Automatic Enrichment" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Every lead is automatically enriched with:
    <ol>
        <li>1. Company information</li>
        <li>2. Contact details</li>
        <li>3. Professional background</li>
        <li>4. Social profiles</li>
    </ol>
  </Card>

  <Card title="Lead Scoring" icon="star">
    Leads are automatically scored based on:
    <ol>
        <li>1. ICP match</li>
        <li>2. Company attributes</li>
        <li>3. Engagement level</li>
        <li>4. Form completion</li>
    </ol>
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Smart Filtering" icon="filter">
    Filter leads by:
    <ol>
        <li>1. Score grade (A, B, C)</li>
        <li>2. Source</li>
        <li>3. Date range</li>
        <li>4. Company size</li>
    </ol>
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
<Card title="Smart Actions" icon="rocket">
    <ol>
        <li>1. Export leads to CSV</li>
        <li>2. Trigger automated workflows</li>
        <li>3. Send to CRM and other integrations</li>
    </ol>
</Card>
