The Surface Tag is a lightweight JavaScript snippet that powers Surface Forms functionality on your website. It enables advanced features like visitor identification, form analytics, and conversion tracking without additional configuration.

## Key Capabilities

  <Card title="Form Embedding" icon="code">

  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Lead De-anonymization" icon="user-secret">

  </Card>

  <Card title="Form Analytics" icon="chart-mixed">

## How It Works

<Steps>
  1. Add the Surface Tag to your website
  2. Tag automatically initializes and connects to Surface
  3. Forms are loaded and tracked automatically
  4. Visitor data is collected and processed
  5. Analytics and insights become available in your dashboard
</Steps>

## Implementation

Add the Surface Tag to your website by including this script in your HTML:

```javascript
<script
  src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/trysurface/scripts@latest/surface_tag.min.js"
  type="application/javascript"
></script>
```
