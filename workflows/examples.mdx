Here are some practical examples of how you can use Surface Forms workflows to automate your lead management process.

## CRM Integration Examples

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Send Lead Data to HubSpot" icon="hubspot">
    Automatically create or update contacts in HubSpot when forms are submitted. Map form fields to HubSpot properties and handle duplicates seamlessly.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Match to CRM Objects" icon="object-group">
    Lead-to-account matching with smart duplicate handling. Match by email domain or company name and set prioritization rules to keep your CRM data clean.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Notification Examples

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Real-time Slack Alerts" icon="slack">
    Send instant form submission notifications to Slack channels. Keep your team informed with customizable message templates including lead details.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Email Notifications" icon="envelope">
    Automatically send email notifications for new leads, form completions, or abandoned forms with customizable templates.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Sales Automation Examples

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="One-Click Follow-up" icon="rocket">
    Automate initial sales response for new leads. Create prospects in your sales tools and trigger follow-up sequences automatically.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Lead Routing by Owner" icon="user-group">
    Route leads to appropriate sales reps based on territory, company size, or industry. Ensure leads are assigned to the right team member.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Lead Management Examples

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Lead Segmentation" icon="layer-group">
    Automatically segment leads based on company size:
    - Enterprise (>1000 employees)
    - Mid-market (100-1000 employees)
    - SMB (< 100 employees)
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Lead Enrichment Flow" icon="database">
    Enrich leads with company and contact data before they enter your CRM. Add company size, industry, and technology data automatically.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Recovery Workflows

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Abandoned Form Recovery" icon="arrow-rotate-left">

  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Meeting No-Shows" icon="calendar-xmark">
